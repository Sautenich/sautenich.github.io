About Me

As a PhD candidate at Skoltech and Invited Researcher at Hong Kong PolyU, I specialize in developing intelligent autonomous systems that combine natural language understanding, vision, and robotics. My mission is to create practical, scalable AI tools that improve real-world operations in healthcare, manufacturing, and aerospace.

  • AI-Powered Mission Control: Architecting Vision-Language-Action (VLA) systems for UAVs capable of understanding natural language instructions and executing them with precision in dynamic environments.
  • Industry-Driven Innovation: Delivered an end-to-end system for furniture board detection using OpenCV, laser guidance, and Azure Kinect, now deployed at a manufacturing facility.
  • Transforming Healthcare with AI: Led the design of an award-winning LLM/VLM system that helps doctors quickly retrieve patient histories from scanned documents—1st Place at a HealthTech Hackathon for improving lives of people with disabilities.

My work sits at the intersection of robotics, deep learning, and computer vision, emphasizing applied AI systems that solve tangible problems. Across academic and industrial projects, I continuously push the boundaries of what AI can achieve in human-centered and resource-constrained settings.
